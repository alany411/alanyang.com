## Overview

As a daily Spotify user, I wanted to see my top artists and tracks without having to wait for Spotify Wrapped. Preferify leverages the Spotify API to fetch a user's top artists and tracks, and can generate playlists based on their recent listening history.

### Technical Overview

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org)
- [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org)
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com)
- [tRPC](https://trpc.io)
- [Auth.js](https://authjs.dev)

## My Role

**Lead Developer and Designer**. As this was a personal project, I was responsible for the entire product lifecycle from conception and design to development and deployment.

## Key Features

- **Top Artists and Tracks** - Fetches and displays a user's top artists and tracks from Spotify.
- **Playlist Generation** - Generates a playlist based on a user's recent listening history.
- **Responsive** - Designed to be used on any device.
- **Accessible** - Compatible with screen readers and keyboard navigation.

## Overview

When the service my team was using become unreliable, I decided to build my own. Pointing Poker is a real-time, collaborative tool that allows my team to estimate, discuss, and align on story points during sprint planning sessions.

### Technical Overview

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org)
- [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org)
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com)
- [Motion](https://motion.dev)
- [Liveblocks](https://liveblocks.io)

## My Role

**Lead Developer and Designer**. As this was a personal project, I was responsible for the entire product lifecycle from conception and design to development and deployment.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time** - Votes are updated as they are cast.
- **Spectator Mode** - Non-participating users can view the current votes.
- **Themes** - Users can choose between light and dark modes.
- **Responsive** - Designed to be used on any device.
- **Accessible** - Compatible with screen readers and keyboard navigation.
